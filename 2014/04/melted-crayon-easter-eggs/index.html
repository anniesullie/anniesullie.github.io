Melted Crayon Easter Eggs

April 19, 2014     Easter

We did Easter eggs for the first time this year. Logan requested that they be “scary”. So Doug and I shredded some crayons and we sprinkled them on the eggs while they were still hot after boiling. We all had a great time, and they look pretty different! Definitely cover the table and floor before trying this, though.
