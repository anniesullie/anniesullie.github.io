Harry Potter Shirt with Stencil Vinyl and Fabric Paint

October 13, 2013     Cricut/Silhouette

I happened upon the Harry P font and decided it would be fun to make a quick shirt for Logan. I typed in some text and drew some footprints in Silhouette Studio, and then printed onto stencil vinyl on my cameo. I used a sponge to paint through the stencil, fading away the footprints.
